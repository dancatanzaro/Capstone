Fudgey Greek Yogurt Brownies

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: ~20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup AP flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • heaping 1/2 cup of plain greek yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Grease a 9x9 inch pan with nonstick spray and line with parchment paper. (To fit the parchment into the pan, place the pan on top of the parchment and cut from the corners of the pan to the edge of the parchment)
  3. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking bowder cocoa powder, sugar, and brown sugar.
  4. Add egg, yogurt, and vanilla exrtract and mix until combined.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Add the mixture to the pan
  7. Bake for 18-22 minutes or internal temperature is 195°F. No thermometer? Insert a toothpick, and if it comes out cleanly, remove brownies from oven.
  8. Let brownies cool for 20 minutes and then lift out using the parchment paper
  9. EAT
