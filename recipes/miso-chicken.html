Grilled Miso Marinated Chicken

Miso paste works great in sauces and marinades and brings a lovely umami note to the party. Miso paste deserves an invite to more of your cooking parties!

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes + at least 3 hours to marinate
  • Cook Time: ~10 + 10 minutes to rest

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 1 teaspoon freshly grated ginger
  • 2 tablespoons white miso
  • 1 tablespoon oil of choice (olive, canola, corn, etc.)
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce (lower sodium, if you please)
  • 1 tablespoon garlic chili paste (sambal olek)

Directions

  1. Put chicken in ziploc bag.
  2. In a bowl, mix remaining ingredients together, and pour marinade onto chicken, moving the bag around to coat the chicken completly; squeeze out excess air and seal.
  3. Let the chicken marinate for at least three hours in the refrigerator.
  4. Heat BBQ grill to high; clean and oil grill grates.
  5. Grill for 4 minutes a side, and check temperature with an instant-read thermometer. When temperature reads 160°F, remove from grill.
  6. Let chicken rest for 10 minutes.
  7. Eat.
