Garlic Soy Sauce Pork Tenderloin

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes + at least 6 hours to marinate
  • Cook Time: 12-15 minutes + 10 minutes resting

Ingredients

  • 1 pork tenderloin (1 to 1½ pounds) trimmed of silver skin and excess fat
  • ⅓ cup soy sauce (reduced sodium works fine)
  • ¼ cup sesame oil
  • ¼ cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 shallot, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon dry mustard
  • 2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • 1½ teaspoons ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Place the pork tenderloin in a ziploc bag.
  2. In a bowl, mix the remaining ingredients together.
  3. Pour the mixture in the bag and seal the bag, removing as much air as possible.
  4. Move the the meat around in order to coat all sides.
  5. Place the bag in a baking dish (just in case the bag leaks) in the refrigerator for at least 6 hours.
  6. Heat grill to highest setting. Once hot, clean and oil grill grates
  7. Place pork tenderloin on grill and give a quarter turn every 2 minutes
  8. Cook until internal temperature reaches 140°F, about 12-15 depending on the size of your pork tenderloin
  9. Let pork rest for 10 minutes before slicing
  10. EAT
