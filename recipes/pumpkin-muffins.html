Pumpkin Tahini Muffins

When I saw this recipe in Feast Magazine , I had to give it a try. These muffins aren't too sweet and provide a unique twist on classic pumpkin muffin recipes. Delicious!

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 24-28 minutes
  • Yield: 12-14 muffins

Ingredients

  • 1 ⅓ cups all-purpose flour
  • ⅓ cups rolled oats
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cloves
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup maple syrup
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup pumpkin puree
  • 2 tablespoons greek yogurt (or sour cream)
  • ¼ cup tahini
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup chopped dates
  • ¼ cup crashed pistachios

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Spray 2 6-cup muffin tins with nonstick spray.
  3. In large mixing bowl, combine first six ingredients.
  4. Mix next 8 ingredients until combined.
  5. Slowly add flour mixture to wet ingredients and blend until combined; fold in dates.
  6. Spoon batter into muffins tins, filling each about 3/4 of the way; sprinkle pistachios on top.
  7. Bake for 24-28 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.
  8. Transfer to a wire rack to cool.
  9. Scarf.
