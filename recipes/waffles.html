Protein Packed Greek Yogurt Waffles

  • Prep Time: 5 minutes
  • Cook Time: ~8 minutes per waffle
  • Yield: 6 waffles

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups of silken tofu (or 6 eggs)
  • 2 cups greek yogurt
  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • pinch of salt
  • dash of milk if batter is too thick

Directions

  1. Plug in your waffle iron and set to medium-high heat.
  2. Pulverize 2 cups of rolled in oats in your favorite blender or food processor.
  3. Put tofu into oat flour and liquify that shit.
  4. Add the remaining ingredients and blend together (it doesn't need to be perfectly smooth).
  5. Spray waffle iron with nonstick cooking spray.
  6. Scoop a half cup and spread out batter on the waffle iron (it's sticky stuff!).
  7. Cook for 4-5 minutes and check on the waffle. The interior may still be gooey. If so, turn down the heat on the waffle iron and cook for a few more minutes.
  8. Eat immediately or chuck em in a bag and store them in the freezer. Reheat in your toaster whenever you're keen for a waffle.
